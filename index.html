Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can sccess them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize tour bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerfull research feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. no need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easilly share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
